FORTH-79 STANDARD DOCUMENT

The attached document is the complete text of the FORTH-79
STANDARD (except for the FORTH-79 HANDY REFERENCE CARD) as
distributed by the Forth Interest Group.

Note: Though all care has been taken to ensure accuracy,
transcription errors may exist.  Should you find an error,
please correct it and send the updated copy to the Taygeta
Forth FTP archive.
